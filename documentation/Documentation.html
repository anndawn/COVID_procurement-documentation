Item cleaning

File

data_analysis/item_department_match/item_cleaned.csv

Method

  • All masks items are cleaned. For the other items, only orders with a payment amount > 0 are cleaned.
  • Items cleaned as the below bolded items.

    Cloth masks (Every order that mentions cloth masks and no other masks)

    Respirator masks ( Every order that mentiones FFP2 and N95 and no other items)

    Uncategorized masks (all other than clothes masks, PPE001, PPE002, respirators)

    Gloves (Every order mentions glove and no other items)

    Gowns (Every order mentions Gown and no other items)

    Thermometer (those mentions Thermometer and no other items; not thermometer battery)

    Apron (Every order that mentions apron and no other items)

    Cleaning services (All orders that indicate cleaning services, not deep cleaning)

    Fumigation and disinfection (All orders indicating fumigation /disinfecting /decontamination / sanitizing/ fogging/ deep cleaning an area or equipment. Exclude PPE021 sanitizing buildings)

    Dispenser (Every order that indicates it's dispenser, not refill)

    Visor / Face Shield (those mention visor or face shield and no other items)

    Uncatgorized sanitizers (All orders that indicate sanitizer, disinfectant, wipes, including refill)
